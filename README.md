## Workshop 2 Plant Something: Buttons, Plots, and Growing
Turn plots into real interactables with simple states (Empty - Seed - Ready). Let the player
press E to plant while standing on a plot, and make interaction readable with a clear
highlight/glow. Make plants feel alive with simple growth + gentle wind controls.

## Workshop 3 Harvest & Earn: Coins, Goals, and Effects
Complete the core loop: harvest Ready plants, earn coins, and track progress toward a win goal.
Add simple UI for coins/harvest count and a basic win screen. Make it satisfying with particle
effects for harvesting, coin gain, and a bigger win celebration.
